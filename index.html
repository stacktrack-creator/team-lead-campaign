My Platform

Three things I'll actually do.

No vague promises. Here's what I'm committing to from day one as team lead.

01
Weekly 1:1s — No Cancellations
Every team member gets protected 1:1 time with me, every week. These don't get cancelled. Your growth, blockers, and ideas come first.
People-first
02
Clear Priorities, Zero Ambiguity
I'll make sure everyone knows what we're working on and why. No more wondering "is this the most important thing?" — I'll handle alignment upward so you can focus downward.
Clarity
03
Remove Blockers Fast
When something's in your way, I'll own fixing it — whether it's another team, a process, or a decision that needs to be made. I won't let bureaucracy slow you down.
Momentum
04
Celebrate the Team's Work
Good work should be seen. I'll make sure contributions get recognized — internally and up the chain. Your wins won't go unnoticed on my watch.
Recognition
05
Honest Feedback, Both Ways
I'll give you real, constructive feedback — not platitudes. And I'll actively ask for yours. I'm here to improve too.
Growth
06
A Team That Feels Like a Team
Stronger relationships = better work. I'll invest in the culture: small rituals, genuine connection, and a space where everyone feels they belong.
Culture
